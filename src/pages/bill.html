What does this bill do?

The bill aims to allow the employer and employee to agree on a Voluntary Work Arrangement which allows the employee to arrange a flexible work week given that the same amount of hours per week is rendered.

Additionally, this bill also ensures the employee that he/she shall receive all existing benefits, including overtime pay, regardless of any work arrangement

How does this affect me?

Directly - As long as your employer agrees or there is a national emergency, you can arrange a different work week schedule as oppose to the usual work week you are accustomed to.
